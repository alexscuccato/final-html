A Little About Me

I am a miniature dachshund named Samwise Gamgee. My parents named me after the aforementioned Samwise from the Lord of the Rings series. Currently I live in Edmonton, Alberta and although it's a little cold, I like it very much.

At first I enjoyed chewing on bones and pooping where I wasn't supposed to. Now I love coding websites for all my furry friends. On the weekends, I enjoy visiting grandma's house and chasing after balls at the dog park.
